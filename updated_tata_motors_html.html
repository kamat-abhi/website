Who We Are

Regium Innovations and Research Pvt. Ltd. is a forward-thinking company committed to engineering the future through innovation, technology, and research. Founded by Mr. Piyush Kumar, our company stands at the intersection of science and practical application, offering transformative solutions across a wide range of industries.

Our mission is to address real-world challenges by blending creativity with deep technical expertise. From modifying high-performance vehicles to purifying water through cutting-edge systems, and from advancing chemical formulations to supporting R&D at scale — we are driven by the pursuit of excellence and impact.

At Regium, we work closely with private industries, government bodies, and individuals to deliver tailored, efficient, and sustainable solutions.
